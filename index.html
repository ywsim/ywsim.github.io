Youngwoo Sim

Hardware Engineer | Roboticist

I am a mechanical engineer with a passion for building robots—where science and art converge. I have designed and built a full-humanoid robot, dexterious hand, whole-body haptic and motion capture interfaces, along with many other systems involving motor drivers, sensors, real-time Linux, and NI LabVIEW + FPGA integrations. More recently, I have been having fun with RT kernels and PCB design with microprocessors.

Before engineering, I was an aspiring classical pianist, winning four 1st-place and one 3rd-place awards in national competitions in Korea. I later shifted toward science and engineering, initially dreaming of becoming a mechanical watch designer. I have always carried both art and engineering in my heart—and that balance is what naturally led me to designing robots.
